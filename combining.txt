Combining Character:
    with the General Category of Combining Mark (M)
    definition D52 in http://www.unicode.org/versions/Unicode5.0.0/ch03.pdf#G30602

Combining Character Sequence
    (base character followed)? by 1+ of combining character, ZERO WIDTH JOINER, or ZERO WIDTH NON-JOINER
    ZERO WIDTH NON-JOINER is 200c
    ZERO WIDTH JOINER is 200d

    Combining Diacritical Marks (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Combining_diacritical_mark and unicode.org PDFs referenced there)

    Combining Diacritical Marks Supplement
	1dc0 - 1dff
    Combining Diacritical Marks for Symbols
	20d0 - 20ff
    Combining Half Marks
